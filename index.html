A Brief Overview of Me

I have been kicking a soccer ball since I could walk, and I currenlty play for the Albion Hurricanes on the U14 ECNL RL team. I will play any position on the field that the team needs, but my preferred position is Left Winger. I have also played Center Forward, Goalkeeper, and Center Attaking Mid(CAM). I love to compete and be on the field or court regardless of the sport. It is my happy place. At school, I really enjoy learning new things and I look forward to going each and every day.
